Kale Caesar Quinoa Salad

Kale and quinoa salad!!

Total time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup kale
  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Caesar Dressing

Preparation

  1. Prepare quinoa and roast until golden brown and 165 in middle.

    Time: 40 minutes

  2. Toss quinoa, kale, and Caesar dressing until coated.

    Time: 4 minutes

  3. Add walnuts and olive oil as garnish.

    Time: 1 minute
